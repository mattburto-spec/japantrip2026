Osaka

Osaka

Street food capital — 3 nights at Holiday Inn Osaka Namba

1
Saturday 20 June

Arrive in Osaka

Arrive at Kansai Airport in the evening. Take the Rapi:t express train to Namba station — it's a fun retro-futuristic train! Check in to the hotel and rest up for the adventure ahead.

Holiday Inn Osaka Namba 3 nights
2
Sunday 21 June

Osaka Castle & Dotonbori

Morning at Osaka Castle — explore the beautiful park and museum inside. In the afternoon, head to Dotonbori, Osaka's most famous street with giant neon signs, lively atmosphere, and incredible street food.

Holiday Inn Osaka Namba
3
Monday 22 June

Kaiyukan Aquarium

Visit one of the world's largest aquariums — home to whale sharks, manta rays, and all sorts of sea life. Then ride the giant Tempozan Ferris Wheel next door for panoramic harbour views.

Holiday Inn Osaka Namba
Hiroshima

Hiroshima

History and island beauty — 2 nights at Sheraton Grand Hiroshima

4
Tuesday 23 June

Hiroshima Peace Memorial

Luggage is shipped ahead to Kyoto — no heavy bags! Take the bullet train (Shinkansen) to Hiroshima. It's fast, smooth, and very comfortable. Visit the moving Peace Memorial Park and Museum.

Sheraton Grand Hiroshima 2 nights
5
Wednesday 24 June

Miyajima Island

Take a short ferry ride to Miyajima Island to see the iconic floating Torii gate — one of Japan's most photographed sights. Option to hike or take the ropeway up Mt Misen for stunning panoramic views.

Sheraton Grand Hiroshima
Kyoto & Nara

Kyoto & Nara

Temples, bamboo, and deer — 4 nights at an Airbnb townhouse in Shimogyo-ku

6
Thursday 25 June

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Bullet train to Kyoto. Pick up forwarded luggage. Then visit Fushimi Inari — walk through thousands of bright vermillion torii gates winding up the mountainside. It's magical.

Airbnb Townhouse, Shimogyo-ku 4 nights
7
Friday 26 June

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Head to the Arashiyama district. Stroll through the towering Bamboo Grove — it feels like another world. Visit the cheeky Monkey Park, and ride the Sagano Scenic Railway through the mountains.

Airbnb Townhouse, Shimogyo-ku
8
Saturday 27 June

Day Trip to Nara

A short train ride to Nara. See the enormous Great Buddha at Todai-ji Temple — truly awe-inspiring. Then feed the hundreds of friendly wild deer that roam freely through the park.

Airbnb Townhouse, Shimogyo-ku
9
Sunday 28 June

Kiyomizu-dera & Nishiki Market

Visit the stunning Kiyomizu-dera Temple perched on a hillside with sweeping views. Walk down the charming Ninenzaka stone-paved steps. End at Nishiki Market for local treats and snacks.

Airbnb Townhouse, Shimogyo-ku
Japanese Alps

Takayama

Mountain charm and ryokan life — 3 nights with breakfast & dinner included

10
Monday 29 June

Scenic Train to Takayama

Luggage shipped ahead to Hokkaido. Take the scenic train via Nagoya up into the Japanese Alps to the charming mountain town of Takayama. The journey is gorgeous — rolling hills, rivers, and valleys.

Takayama Ouan Ryokan 3 nights · meals incl.
11
Tuesday 30 June

Morning Market & Old Town

Browse the Miyagawa Morning Market for local crafts and produce. Wander Sanmachi Suji — beautifully preserved Edo-era streets with sake breweries and craft shops. Try the famous Hida beef for lunch!

Takayama Ouan Ryokan
12
Wednesday 1 July

Shirakawa-go Village

Day trip by bus to Shirakawa-go — a UNESCO World Heritage village famous for its traditional thatched-roof farmhouses. Like stepping back in time. Truly one of Japan's most beautiful spots.

Takayama Ouan Ryokan
Hokkaido

Hokkaido

Lavender, wildlife, and wide open spaces — 8 nights across Furano & Sapporo

13
Thursday 2 July

Fly to Hokkaido

Train to Nagoya, then fly to Sapporo (departs 12:35, arrives 14:20 — Japan Airlines). Pick up the rental car and drive to Furano. Collect forwarded luggage at the house.

Airbnb House, Furano 4 nights
14
Friday 3 July

Farm Tomita Lavender Fields

Visit Farm Tomita — famous for its stunning lavender fields in peak season! Rolling purple as far as you can see. Then explore Ningle Terrace, a woodland village of adorable log cabin craft shops.

Airbnb House, Furano
15
Saturday 4 July

Blue Pond & Patchwork Road

Scenic driving day through Biei. See the vivid Blue Pond (Aoiike) — an otherworldly turquoise pond with white birch trees. Visit Shirahige Waterfall and cruise the rolling Patchwork Road through flower fields.

Airbnb House, Furano
16
Sunday 5 July

Asahiyama Zoo

Drive to Asahiyama Zoo — famous across Japan for its unique walk-through exhibits. Watch penguins parade, polar bears swim overhead, and see animals up close in naturalistic settings.

Airbnb House, Furano
17
Monday 6 July

Otaru Canals & on to Sapporo

Drive from Furano to the charming port town of Otaru. Stroll along the picturesque stone-lined canals and visit the enchanting Music Box Museum. Continue to Sapporo and return the rental car.

Bespoke Hotel Sapporo 4 nights
18
Tuesday 7 July

Sapporo Beer Museum

Visit the iconic Sapporo Beer Museum and try "Genghis Khan" grilled lamb BBQ — a Hokkaido specialty. Then explore the covered Tanukikoji Shopping Arcade for souvenirs and snacks.

Bespoke Hotel Sapporo
19
Wednesday 8 July

Shiroi Koibito Park & Mt Moiwa

Tour Shiroi Koibito Park — a whimsical chocolate factory where you can watch sweets being made. Take the Mt Moiwa Ropeway for panoramic city views. Optional: Nikka Whisky Yoichi Distillery.

Bespoke Hotel Sapporo
20
Thursday 9 July

Moerenuma Park

A relaxed day at Moerenuma Park — a beautifully designed sculpture park by artist Isamu Noguchi. Great for a peaceful walk. Last chance for souvenir shopping before heading home!

Bespoke Hotel Sapporo
21
Friday 10 July

Heading Home

Morning free for last-minute souvenirs or a relaxing breakfast. Then fly from Sapporo (CTS) via Osaka (KIX) back home to Sydney. What an adventure!
